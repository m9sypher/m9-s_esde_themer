KNOWN TIPS / GOTCHAS

- For a plain image or video, the fit behavior comes entirely from which size property you use: size stretches to exact dimensions, maxSize preserves aspect ratio and shrinks to fit, cropSize (+ optional cropPos) scales up and crops to fill. There's no separate "imageFit" property for these two — that one's real, but only on carousel/grid items.
- Adding a new variant through Capabilities on an existing theme auto-wires it into theme.xml on Save — that patch only happens on Save, not automatically as you type.
- Testing live while ES-DE is open: switch to a different Variant and back to force it to reload your edit.
- View names are only ever "gamelist", "system", or "all" — different looks are separate variant files, not view names.
- Colors are RRGGBBAA, no leading # — handled automatically now.
