M9 THEME EDITOR — CHANGELOG

────────────────────────────

Preview resolution: a real resolution dropdown (Steam Deck, 720p/1080p/1440p/4K, 4:3, 5:4, etc.) instead of just a ratio toggle, plus a Resolution Declarations window to add your own custom resolutions — all preview-only, confirmed to never affect saved XML, Compile, or Save to disk in any way.

Bug Report: a button linking straight to the ESDE Theme Editor section of a public Discord — report issues, see updates, or show off a theme.

────────────────────────────

KNOWN TIPS / GOTCHAS

- Testing live while ES-DE is open: switch to a different Variant and back to force it to reload your edit.
- View names are only ever "gamelist", "system", or "all" — different looks are separate variant files, not view names.
- Colors are RRGGBBAA, no leading # — handled automatically now.
- cornerRadius is for images/rectangles; video uses videoCornerRadius / imageCornerRadius.
